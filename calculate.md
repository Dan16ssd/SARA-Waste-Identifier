# Project S.A.R.A. — TAM / SAM / SOM Calculation Breakdown

**Figures used in the pitch deck (Section 11 of info.md):**
- TAM: $4.2 Billion

- SOM: $2.1 Million

---

## TAM — $4.2 Billion (Global Campus Sustainability Tech)

**Method:** top-down from Smart Campus market, filtered to sustainability function.

| Input | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Global Smart Campus Market (2024) | $10.56 Billion | WiseGuy Reports — Smart Campus Market Research 2035 |
| Sustainability/environmental management share of smart campus spend | ~40% | (energy, waste, water, carbon monitoring sub-segments) |

**Calculation:**
```
$10.56B × 40% = $4.224B ≈ $4.2B TAM
```

**Why 40%?** Smart campus platforms cover security, building automation, transport, learning tech, and sustainability. Market breakdowns consistently show energy + environment + waste as the largest combined segment (~35–45% of smart campus spend).

---

## SAM — $380 Million (Southeast Asia Sustainability Tech)

**Method:** top-down from Asia-wide green tech market, filtered to Southeast Asia's share.

| Input | Value | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Asia green technology & sustainability market (2024) | $6,415.9 Million | PS Market Research — Asia Green Technology and Sustainability Market Report |
| Southeast Asia's share of Asia sustainability tech spend | ~6% | SEA's approximate share of Asia's GDP and digital investment in sustainability verticals |

**Calculation:**
```
$6,415.9M × 6% = $384.9M ≈ $380M SAM
```

**Why 6%?** Southeast Asia (10 ASEAN countries) represents approximately 6–7% of Asia's total economic output. Applied to the Asia sustainability-tech market, this yields a conservative SEA-specific figure. The real number may be higher as SEA governments are accelerating green mandates (see Lao PDR NDC 2021, ASEAN Smart Cities Network).

## SOM — $2.1 Million (Laos + Greater Mekong Subregion, Realistic Year 1–3)

**Method:** bottom-up from SARA's actual pricing tiers × addressable customer counts in the reachable GMS geography.

### Step 1 — Count addressable customers in GMS (excluding China)

| Country | Universities / HEIs | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Laos | ~117 | Wikipedia — List of universities in Laos |
| Vietnam | ~240 | UniRank |
| Thailand | ~170 | UniRank |
| Cambodia | ~130 | UniRank |
| Myanmar | ~170 | UniRank |
| **GMS Total (ex-China)** | **~827** | |
| Realistic reachable (language + connectivity filter) | **~700** | |

Municipality districts reachable in GMS capital cities + provincial centers: **~200**

Enterprise / ESG-focused facilities (factories, hotels, campuses with sustainability mandates): **~50**

### Step 2 — Apply SARA's pricing tiers

| Tier | Price | Addressable customers | Annual revenue if 100% captured |
|---|---|---|---|
| University | $99 / month | 700 universities | $700 × $99 × 12 = **$831,600** |
| Municipality | $299 / month | 200 districts | $200 × $299 × 12 = **$717,600** |
| Enterprise | $999 / month | 50 facilities | $50 × $999 × 12 = **$599,400** |
| **Total SOM** | | | **$2,148,600 ≈ $2.1M** |
